TALLAHASSEE TO GAINESVILLE

PATH TO COMPUTER ENGINEERING

As a child I was always interested in art and building LEGOs. I originally thought that I would take those two passions of mine and become an architect.

Once in high school, I began taking drafting classes to learn more about design and AutoCAD. As a consequence of this, I realized that I could use my new skills to become a mechanical engineer, which I felt was a more sound career choice.

Once at the University of Florida I became involved in the Audio Engineering Society where I was surrounded by ECE students. My involvement led to realizing Computer Engineering best aligned with my interests.

In my spare time I like to get involved in student organizations such as the Audio Engineering Society and the National Society of Black Engineers Gator Chapter.

Outside of school, I enjoy critically analyzing music, learning instruments, and writing songs as well as other more recreational activities such as skateboarding, basketball, and video games.
